My Story

I am a very energetic and inquisitive individual with a passion for all things design related. I have a key interest in creating engaging and successful product experiences through first understanding the end user. This is reinforced through my decision to retraining and pivot into the field of UX Design. Going forwards, I aim to continue to hone my skills so as to become a better designer, leader and overall teammate.
